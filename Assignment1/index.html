Shahriar Manzoor

Judge of ACM-ICPC World Finals.

Here's a time line of Shahriar Manzoor's life:

  • 1976 - Born in Chittagong, Bangladesh.
  • 1999 - He took part in the ACM ICPC Dhaka Site in the year as a part of a team from BUET and his team stood third.
  • 2003 & 2004 - Chief Judge of National Programming Contest.
  • 2004 – 2017 He is also the Judging Director of ACM ICPC Dhaka Site and chief judge of ACM ICPC Kuala lumpur Regional Contest 2010.
  • 2003–2018 He is a Judge of ACM-ICPC World Finals.
  • He is currently the Chairman of Department of CSE of Southeast University
